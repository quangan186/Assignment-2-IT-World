Team Profile

Bui Quang An

According to my Myers- Briggs test result, my advantage is that I am an extraverted, observant and assertive person. The extravert helps me be trustworthy in other eyes and easy to find support from others when I have difficulty. On the other hand, observation helps me learn and gain more knowledge and life’s experiences so that I can be more assertive when I make any decisions. These strengths help me connect members in my team, make logical decisions, and support members while we are doing projects. However, I need to find 3 members whose strengths are writing reports, collecting and checking report’s mistakes, and designing not only the report but also the slides for the presentation. Luckily, even though it is quite difficult to find teammates this semester because of Covid pandemic, I have found and invited enough teammates whose strengths are similar to what I need after 6 weeks. They are Duong My Uyen, Pham Phuc Binh and Nguyen Phuoc Thanh.

Pham Phuc Binh

Binh is the oldest member in my team in the User-centred Design (UCD) course last semester and his strength is writing reports, which has been witnessed in his final exam , with 88 points, and he is willing to help other members when they have difficulty in writing reports.

Nguyen Phuoc Thanh

Thanh is an introverted boy in my group, but from my friends’ feedback working with him last semester, he has a creative mind and high aesthetic, which is necessary for designing the report.

Duong My Uyen

Last but not least, Uyen is the only girl in my group and she is a polite and enthusiastic person. However, Uyen's Myers- Briggs test result demonstrates that her personality is almost opposite of mine. For instance, Uyen’s result shows that she is an introverted person , and she uses her feelings to make decisions, with 62 percent of introverts and 64 percent of feelings, while I am an extravert and use thinking to make decisions, about 70 percent of extravert and 52 percent of thinking. In my opinion, it is very interesting to me because it helps me consider carefully before making any decisions for the group. Also, with the advantages that she used to study overseas a long time ago, I believe letting her synthesize our work in the report and check mistakes such as content or grammar is suitable.
